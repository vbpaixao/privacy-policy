# Privacy Policy for RedactPhoto

### Privacy Policy Overview

At RedactPhoto, we prioritize your privacy. Our commitment is to ensure that your personal data remains just that - personal. We don’t collect, track, sell, or distribute your personal information, and we do not integrate advertising networks into our app. Our revenue is generated through the sale of software licenses, upholding our principle of not compromising user privacy for profit.

### Introduction & Consent

This document explains how the RedactPhoto app manages personal information. By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this privacy policy.

### Our Approach to Privacy

We create our products with your privacy in mind. This means processing only anonymous usage data to improve our services.

### Distribution via App Store

Our iOS apps rely on Apple‑provided tools (e.g., crash reports and analytics). Purchases and subscriptions are processed by Apple via your Apple ID. For details on how Apple handles data, see [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/en-ww/).

### Data Collection Practices

RedactPhoto does not collect or store your personal information on our servers. All data is securely stored on your device. For details on how data is handled, see [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/en-ww/).

When it comes to in‑app purchases through the App Store, we work with RevenueCat to handle the process. RevenueCat collects anonymized data necessary for smooth in‑app transactions. This data includes anonymous user IDs, whether a purchase was made, the country of purchase, and when the transactions occurred. To learn more, see [RevenueCat's Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

### Policy Updates

We may change this privacy policy in the future. Make sure to check this page now and then to keep up with the rules that apply to your use of the RedactPhoto app.

#### Contact

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding this privacy policy, do not hesitate to [contact us](./contact.html).
